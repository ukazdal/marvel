About Us

Marvel Entertainment is an entertainment company based in the United States. The company was founded as a result of the merger of Marvel Entertainment Group and ToyBiz on June 2, 1998, and was acquired by The Walt Disney Company in 2009. The headquarters of the company is located in New York.
Marvel Entertainment is today a subsidiary of The Walt Disney Company, especially known for Marvel Comics, Marvel Animation and Marvel Television. Formerly owned by Marvel, Marvel Studios has now become a direct subsidiary of The Walt Disney Studios.
